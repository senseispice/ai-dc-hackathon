STATEMENT OF WORK
EcoRestore National Corp
Silver Creek Abandoned Mine Land Reclamation Project
BLM-NV-2026-0042

1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
EcoRestore National Corp is a leading national environmental services firm providing comprehensive restoration and remediation solutions across the United States. With over 500 employees and offices in 12 states, EcoRestore has the resources and capacity to deliver projects of any scale. We have completed numerous environmental restoration projects for federal, state, and private clients.

2. TECHNICAL APPROACH

2.1 Site Characterization
EcoRestore will conduct a thorough assessment of the Silver Creek site to determine current conditions and develop our restoration strategy. Our experienced team will evaluate soil conditions, water quality, existing vegetation, and mine features to create a comprehensive understanding of the site.

2.2 Remediation and Stabilization
We will implement proven remediation techniques to address mine hazards and environmental contamination at the site. Mine openings will be permanently closed. Waste rock will be regraded and stabilized. We will install appropriate water treatment infrastructure to address acid mine drainage issues, drawing on our national experience with similar projects.

2.3 Site Restoration
Following remediation, EcoRestore will restore the site to a productive ecological condition. Our approach includes:
- Application of soil amendments and growth media to support plant establishment
- Installation of erosion control measures including biodegradable blankets, hydroseeding, and check dams
- Seeding with a grass and forb mix designed for rapid ground cover establishment
- Planting of native shrubs where appropriate
- Use of annual ryegrass and cereal rye as nurse crops to provide immediate erosion protection while native species establish

We will source seed from established national suppliers who can guarantee availability and purity.

2.4 Erosion Control
Comprehensive erosion control using industry-standard BMPs including hydroseeding with tackifier, erosion control blankets, and temporary sediment basins.

3. MONITORING
EcoRestore will monitor the site during the establishment period to ensure restoration targets are being met. Our monitoring approach includes visual inspections, photo documentation, and periodic vegetation assessments. We will provide annual summary reports to BLM documenting site conditions and any maintenance activities performed.

Monitoring will be conducted for 18 months following completion of planting activities.
